I'm a Conservation and Resource Studies student at UC Berkeley (Rausser College Honors Program, GPA 3.9), graduated in May 2026 with a Geospatial Information Science & Technology minor. My work has been focused on the intersection of environmental science, spatial analysis, and policy.

My honors research uses satellite imagery and GIS to analyze climate and land-use-driven change in the Sacramento–San Joaquin Delta — Performing Oject Based Image Analysis’ on multi-temporal imagery in ArcGIS Pro and processessing validated results in R to identify landscape patterns and environmental trends.

At WWF, I designed interactive an StoryMap with geospatial dashboards to communicate international geospatial initiatives to various stakeholders.
